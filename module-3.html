Video Lesson

Reading: Common Veterinary Diseases

This module covers common diseases encountered in veterinary practice, their symptoms, diagnosis, and treatment protocols.

Infectious Diseases

Infectious diseases are caused by pathogens such as bacteria, viruses, and fungi. Common examples include parvovirus, distemper, and kennel cough.

Parasitic Conditions

Parasites can affect various body systems. Common parasites include fleas, ticks, heartworms, and intestinal worms.
