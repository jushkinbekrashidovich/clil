Frequently Asked Questions

How can I access the course materials?

All course materials are freely available on this website. Simply navigate to the Courses section to access modules, or visit Resources to download materials.

Is this platform suitable for beginners?

Yes, the platform is designed for students at various levels. Each module includes scaffolding and support materials to help learners at different proficiency levels.

Can I use these materials for teaching?

Absolutely! The For Teachers section provides lesson plans, methodological recommendations, and assessment tools specifically designed for educators.

How often is new content added?

We regularly update the platform with new resources, exercises, and content. Check the News section for the latest updates and announcements.
