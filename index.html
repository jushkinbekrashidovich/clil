Welcome to CLIL for Veterinarians

This platform is designed to support veterinary students and professionals in developing their English language skills through Content and Language Integrated Learning (CLIL). CLIL is an innovative educational approach that combines language learning with subject-specific content.

Our comprehensive curriculum covers essential veterinary topics while simultaneously improving English proficiency. Whether you're a student preparing for international practice or a teacher looking for effective teaching materials, this platform provides the tools you need.

The methodology is based on extensive research and follows best practices in both language education and veterinary science. Each module is carefully designed to balance content knowledge with language acquisition.
