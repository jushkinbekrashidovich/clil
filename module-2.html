Reading: Animal Body Systems

Understanding animal anatomy is fundamental to veterinary practice. This module covers the major body systems and their functions.

Skeletal System

The skeletal system provides structure, protection, and support. Key bones include the skull, vertebrae, ribs, and limbs.

Muscular System

Muscles enable movement and maintain posture. Understanding muscle anatomy is essential for surgical procedures.
